About Us

The department's IEEE Club is a student branch of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, the world's largest technical professional organization.

  • Biggest Community

    Over 400,000 members in more than 160 countries, more than 60 percent of whom are from outside the United States.

  • Students & Sections

    More than 107,000 Student members & 342 Sections in ten geographic Regions worldwide.

  • Student Branch

    3,485 Student Branches at colleges and universities in over 100 countries.
